Auditioning

When auditioning don't overdo the warm-up. Overdoing your warm-up can wear out your chops. After warming up you go over snippits of music that you are auditioning on is doing well. After you play snippits of your music rest a bit. You want to be at your best when playing so don't rest to long.

Band Role

Your role in the band varies off the song. You might be a support or main melody.
